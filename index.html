      What is .tinker?

      .tinker is like the source code of your 3D project. Unlike other formats that just save the 3D geometry, .tinker saves everything:

      All 3D objects and their positions
      Materials, colors, and textures
      Groups and constraints
      Your project settings
      Undo/redo history

      Use .tinker to save and restore your work anytime with full editability. Perfect for working on projects over time!
